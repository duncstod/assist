description: Add a new stakeholder profile to ASSIST.md

Read ~/.claude/ASSIST.md to see existing stakeholders.

## Ask

"Who do you want to add?"

Wait for name.

## Gather Info

Ask one at a time:

1. "What's their role/title?"
2. "What do they care about? What are their priorities?"
3. "How should you communicate with them? Any preferences?"
4. "What's your history with them so far?"
5. "What should you always have ready when meeting them?"

## Update ~/.claude/ASSIST.md

Add new stakeholder to STAKEHOLDERS section:

```markdown
## [Name]
- **Role:** [Their answer]
- **Priorities:** [Their answer]
- **Communication style:** [Their answer]
- **Your history:** [Their answer]
- **Prep focus:** [Their answer]
```

## Close

"Added [Name] to your stakeholders. They'll now appear in meeting prep context."
